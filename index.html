My Creative Process

lightbulb

1. The Spark (Ideation & Emotion)

Every project begins with a human core: a question, a feeling, a story. What emotion do I want to evoke? What narrative do I want to explore? This is the soul of the work, the guiding star for the technology that follows.

data_object

2. The Dialogue (AI Collaboration)

Here, I engage in a dialogue with the AI. I select models, curate datasets, and fine-tune parameters. It's a dance of prompts and outputs, a collaboration where I steer the machine's vast potential towards my artistic vision.

palette

3. The Refinement (Human Curation)

AI generates possibilities; I provide the purpose. I curate, edit, and composite the AI's output, infusing it with my own artistic sensibility. This human touch transforms raw generation into a polished, intentional piece of art.
